What is SheetPro?

SheetPro is a suite of enterprise tools that extend Smartsheet's capabilities. It includes advanced forms, client portals, visual automations, and enhanced charting—everything power users need.

Who is it for?

SheetPro is built for Smartsheet power users, project managers, and operations teams who have hit the platform's native limits and need more robust features without leaving the ecosystem.

How does it integrate with Smartsheet?

SheetPro connects directly to your Smartsheet account via API. It reads your sheets and allows you to build interfaces and automations on top of your existing data instantly.

Our separate Portal Builder lets you create secure, branded interfaces for clients or vendors. They log in to a SheetPro portal to view/edit specific data you share, controlled by granular permissions.

Can I use this with existing sheets?

Absolutely! SheetPro enhances your existing Smartsheet sheets. You don't need to migrate data or change how you work; just point SheetPro to your current sheets.

Does this replace Smartsheet?

No, SheetPro is a companion toolkit. You continue to use Smartsheet as your database and core work management platform, while SheetPro handles the "excessive" needs like advanced forms or external portals.
